## Module 01 – Intro to AI in Simple Language

This module gives you a clear starting point:
- What AI and large language models (LLMs) are.
- What they are good at, and where they are weak or risky.
- Where AI can help in real life, work, and study.

### Episodes in this module

Hebrew (`../hebrew/` folder):
- `../hebrew/README.md` – general description of the module (Hebrew).  
- `../hebrew/01_what_is_ai.md` – intuitive explanation of what AI / LLMs are. (~10–15 min, beginner)  
- `../hebrew/02_what_ai_can_and_cannot_do.md` – what AI can do well, and what it cannot. (~10–15 min, beginner)  
- `../hebrew/03_use_cases.md` – practical use cases in life, work and study (with a few psychology/health examples). (~10–20 min, beginner)  
- `../hebrew/04_myths_and_fears.md` – common myths and fears about AI, and how to think about them. (~10–15 min, beginner)

English (this folder):
- `01_what_is_ai.md` – what AI and language models are, in simple English. (~10–15 min, beginner)  
- `02_what_ai_can_and_cannot_do.md` – strengths and limitations of AI. (~10–15 min, beginner)  
- `03_use_cases.md` – everyday and research use-cases. (~10–20 min, beginner)  
- `04_myths_and_fears.md` – common myths and fears, in English. (~10–15 min, beginner)

Use this README as a map: you can start in Hebrew or in English, and move between them if you are comfortable in both languages.
